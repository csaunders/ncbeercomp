Niagara College Winter 2016 Beer Competition

Full Competition Details

It’s time for our winter beer competition! Beers will once again be blind judged using the 2015 Beer Judge Certification Program (BJCP) guidelines. Winners of each category will be announced at the Project Brew event in Market Square, St. Catharines on Friday, April 8, and the judges’ scoring sheets will be returned to every entrant.

Who can enter?

This competition is open to:

  • Home-brewers and home-brewing clubs
  • Niagara College Brewmaster students

Important Dates

Submit entry no later than Monday March 21st at 1700hrs

Winners will be announced on Friday April 8th @ Market Square in St. Catharines

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Is there an entry limit? No
  • How do I enter? Fill out an entry form for each beer submitted and drop off your entries at one of our entry locations
  • What standards will be used to judge the beers? Beers will be judged using the BJCP 2015 guidelines*
  • How will prizes be awarded? Prizes will be awarded per category. For instance, of all the Scottish Ales entered in sub-categories 14A, 14B and 14C, the beer with the highest score in all three subcategories would receive the prize for best Category 14 Scottish Ale.
  • Will I get judges comments on my beer? All scoresheets with comments and scoring will be returned to competitors. If you are not a student ensure that your complete contact information is on the form

Category Submission Guidelines

  • If your beer is in categories 1-27, only submit the first entry form (page 3 of the competition details PDF).
  • If your beer is in categories 28-34, only submit the second entry form (page 4 of the competition details PDF).
